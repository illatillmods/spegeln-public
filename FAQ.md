# FAQ

## Is Spegeln open source?

No. This mirror is a public review and transparency surface only. It does not
grant permission to copy, modify, host, resell or rebuild the product.

## Is the source code included?

No. Source code, prompts, schemas, datasets, deployment configuration and
private operational notes are deliberately excluded.

## Is reporting anonymous?

Public language should say pseudonymous or review-gated unless a signed legal
policy says otherwise. The private product includes abuse-prevention and review
mechanisms that can involve fingerprints, sender metadata or account context.

## Is there a public API?

The product includes a scoped API surface with keys, scopes and rate limits.
Premium, partner or bulk access is a separate review path.

## Are there benchmarks?

No public benchmark results are included in this mirror.

## Are there external security certifications?

No external SOC 2, ISO 27001 or equivalent certification is claimed here.

## Can I contribute code?

No public source contributions are accepted through this mirror. Product
feedback, press requests or partnership discussions should go through the project
owner's chosen contact channel.
